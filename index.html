A Dev Conference for a better community by an awesome community.

Our Mission

Our goal is to push the conversation of software development beyond the basics and push innovation in design, architecture, data storage, and technical leadership. We believe an annual conference is a way to begin these discussions that can carry over to our developer community, businesses, and organizations throughout the year to improve ourselves and by extension, our community.

Code of Conduct

We are dedicated to providing a harassment-free conference experience for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion (or lack thereof), financial status, political views, or technology choices. We do not tolerate harassment of conference participants in any form. Sexual language and imagery is not appropriate for any venue, talk, workshop, party, or online media. Participants violating these rules may be expelled from the conference without a refund at the discretion of the conference organisers.
