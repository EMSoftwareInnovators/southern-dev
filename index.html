Our Mission

Our goal is to push the conversation of software development beyond the basics and push innovation in design, architecture, data storage, and technical leadership. By partnering with local and regional partners, we believe that an annual conference is a way to begin these discussions that can carry over to our developer community, businesses, and organizations throughout the year to improve ourselves and by extension, our community.
